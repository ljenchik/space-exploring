Sojourner

The first wheeled vehicle to rove on a planet other than Earth

Landed: July, 1997

Worked on Mars for 95 days

Mission: To test in the Martian environment technical solutions that were developed by engineers of the NASA research laboratories

Spirit and Opportunity

Twin robotic rovers lived well beyond their planned 90-day missions

Landed: January, 2004

Opportunity worked on Mars for 14 years and 138 days, Spirit for 6 years and 77 days

Mission: To find evidence of water on Mars

Spirit and Opportunity

Curiosity

Curiosity is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Gale crater on Mars

Landed: August, 2012

Worked on Mars for: 10 years and 185 days

Mission: To find out if Mars once had what all life needs, lasting water and the right chemicals

Curiosity

Perseverance

Landed: February, 2021

Perseverance is similar to Curiosity and carries a small robotic helicopter

Currently is operating on Mars

Mission: To seek out evidence of former life, collect rock and soil samples, and test oxygen production from the Martian atmosphere

Perseverance
